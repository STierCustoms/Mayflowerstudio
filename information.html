Information & Terms

Ordering information and preview terms

These terms are included for the preview version and can be refined once final business policies are confirmed.

1. Ordering Notice

All bouquet orders require a minimum of 3 days’ notice unless otherwise agreed in writing. Pre-booked orders may be placed further in advance.

2. Florist Choice Designs

Florist choice arrangements are made according to the selected colour palette, style and size. Exact flower varieties may vary, as these pieces are created freely and depend on seasonal availability.

3. Seasonal Substitutions

We reserve the right to substitute stems or varieties where necessary due to seasonal changes, supply limitations or quality concerns. Any substitutions will remain in keeping with the selected style, colour palette and overall design feel.

4. Bouquet Sizes

Bouquet sizes are offered in Small, Medium and Large. The visual fullness and stem count may vary between designs, but the selected size will determine the overall value and scale of the arrangement.

5. Vase Add-On

Customers may choose to add a vase to eligible bouquet orders. Vase pricing may vary depending on the selected bouquet size.

6. Weddings, Events & Corporate Enquiries

Event, wedding and corporate bookings are bespoke and subject to consultation, availability and quotation. Pricing shown online is for guidance only where stated.

7. Amendments & Cancellations

Once an order has been confirmed, amendments may only be possible with reasonable notice and subject to availability. Bespoke or event-based floral work may be subject to separate cancellation terms.

8. Delivery & Collection

Delivery and collection terms will be confirmed directly with the client. Specific timing and availability may vary depending on location and order type.

9. Imagery

Website imagery is intended as a visual guide. Final arrangements may differ slightly in composition while preserving the intended look and feel.

10. Preview Website Notice

This current version of the website is a design preview. Content, imagery, product names, prices, contact details and policy wording may be updated before final launch.
